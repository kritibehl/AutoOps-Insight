# AI Product Support Incident Intelligence

AutoOps models AI product failures as structured support incidents.

## Supported issue families

- wrong_answer
- missing_context
- tool_failure
- retrieval_failure
- latency_spike
- unsafe_response

## Generated stakeholder outputs

Each support incident can produce:

- PM summary
- Engineering bug report
- Support action plan

## Scale simulation proof

The synthetic generator creates support incidents across multiple product/source systems and produces:

- support volume by category
- recurring customer blockers
- escalation count
- action distribution
- stakeholder-ready summaries

## Intended use

This layer supports launch-readiness reviews, AI product support workflows, and recurring issue analysis.
